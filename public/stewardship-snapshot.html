Recommended Path Forward

Overall Suitability:

 

Institution Name appears to be a candidate for a Covenant Charge EV fast-charging program. If your leadership decides to move forward, here is what the process looks like:

1
Program Agreement

Before any work begins, we establish a formal program agreement between Institution Name and Covenant Charge. This document defines our role as your long-term representative, confirms there is no cost to your institution, and protects your decision-making authority throughout the process. Nothing moves forward without your leadership's signature and comfort.

2
Engineering Pre-Assessment

Once an agreement is in place, a licensed electrical engineer reviews your existing electrical service to confirm capacity and scope any required upgrades. Covenant Charge coordinates this process on your behalf.

3
Operator Selection

Covenant Charge introduces your institution to vetted charging operators aligned with your site profile. We manage that relationship and help you evaluate terms — you make the final decision.

4
Financing & Contracts

With an operator selected, we work through equipment financing and purchase contracts. Covenant Charge stays involved to ensure your interests are protected.

5
Site Design & Engineering

A licensed engineer designs the site layout — charger placement, electrical routing, ADA compliance, and conduit pathways. Covenant Charge coordinates this process. The design is submitted to your local jurisdiction and utility for permitting approval before any construction begins.

6
Construction & Installation

A licensed electrical contractor builds out the site per the approved design. Covenant Charge provides oversight throughout construction and manages the relationship with the operator and contractor on your behalf.

7
Commissioning & Launch

Once installed, the equipment is tested, activated, and connected to the operator's network. Your site goes live. Covenant Charge remains your long-term representative from this point forward — monitoring performance, managing operator relationships, and keeping your leadership informed.

About Covenant Charge

Covenant Charge is a faith-forward EV charging platform that helps faith-based institutions responsibly unlock long-term, sustainable revenue from underutilized parking assets. We serve as a long-term program representative — not a one-time contractor — providing stewardship, oversight, and continuity as the EV industry evolves. We do not own equipment, take on operational risk, or charge institutions for our services.
